Instructions for Minecraft

Press any arrow key to make the boy appear. Move him around the board using the arrow keys. Click any of the below keys to make blocks.

Shift + P increases the block size
Shift + M decreases the block size
W makes a wall block
G makes a ground block
L makes a light green block
T makes a trunk block
R makes a roof block
Y makes a yellow wall block
D makes a dark green block
C makes a cloud block
U makes a unique block

Reload the page to clear the board and restart your creation.
